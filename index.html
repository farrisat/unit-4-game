Dragon Catcher!

How to Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four dragons below. By clicking on a dragon you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above that random number.

The value of each dragon is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the values of each dragon will change. Good luck!
